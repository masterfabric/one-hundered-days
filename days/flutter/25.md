## üìö Dictionary for Developers

*   **Review of Previous Terms:**
    *   **`Row`:** Arranges children horizontally.
    *   **`Column`:** Arranges children vertically.
    *   **`Stack`:** Overlays children on top of each other.
    *   **`Expanded`:** Makes a child fill available space.
    *   **`Flexible`:** Allows a child to fill space but can be smaller.
    *   **Sliver:** A portion of a scrollable area for custom scroll effects.
