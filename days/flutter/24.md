## ğŸ“– Task Explanations

Slivers are a more advanced concept in Flutter layout, but they are incredibly powerful for creating custom scrolling experiences. Unlike regular `ListView` or `GridView` widgets, which scroll as a single unit, slivers are individual scrollable areas that can be combined within a `CustomScrollView`.

This allows you to create effects like a collapsing app bar (`SliverAppBar`), where the app bar shrinks or expands as you scroll through the content. `SliverList` and `SliverGrid` are the sliver equivalents of `ListView` and `GridView`, designed to work seamlessly within a `CustomScrollView`.

*   **Sliver:** A portion of a scrollable area. Slivers can be combined in a `CustomScrollView` to create custom scroll effects.
*   **`CustomScrollView`:** A scrollable widget that creates custom scroll effects using slivers.
*   **`SliverAppBar`:** An app bar that integrates with a `CustomScrollView` to create effects like collapsing and expanding.
*   **`SliverList`:** A sliver that displays a linear list of children.
*   **`SliverGrid`:** A sliver that displays a grid of children.
