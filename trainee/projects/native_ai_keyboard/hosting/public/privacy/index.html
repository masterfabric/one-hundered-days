In short We built AI Keyboard to help you write better on your iPhone. We need a small amount of information to run the app and AI tools. We do not sell your data, we do not show ads based on your messages, and we do not offer subscriptions in the app today. You can contact us anytime with questions or requests about your information.

This Privacy Policy tells you what information Masterfabric (“we”, “us”) collects when you use AI Keyboard on iPhone, including the keyboard you add in Settings. By using the app, you agree to this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the app.

Who is responsible for your information?

Masterfabric is the data controller for AI Keyboard. You can reach us through our Support page or at support@masterfabric.com.

What information we use and why

  • To make the app work on your phone. We use a device-related identifier so our systems know your installation and can provide AI features. You do not need to create an account or give us your name or email to use the keyboard.
  • When you ask for AI help. If you tap actions such as rewrite, proofread, shorten, or expand, the text you selected is sent securely to our servers and processed by AI to give you a suggestion. We use that text only to provide the feature, keep the service safe, and fix problems—not to advertise to you. We do not keep your message text longer than we need for those purposes.
  • When you contact us in the app. If you use Report a problem, we receive what you write plus basic details (for example app version and language) so we can respond and improve the app.
  • Your choices in the app. Settings such as writing style and keyboard look are stored on your device and shared between the main app and the keyboard so they stay in sync.
  • To improve stability. We use Google’s Firebase services for crash reports and general, anonymous usage information (for example which screens are used). These tools are not meant to collect the content of your messages or AI requests.

Allow Full Access on iPhone

Apple only lets custom keyboards use the internet if you turn on Allow Full Access. Without it, AI features that need the internet will not work. When you enable it, Apple shows you its own privacy message. We only send information over the internet when you use a feature that needs it (for example an AI action or sending a problem report from the main app).

Who we share information with

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers that help us run the app, under contracts that require them to protect your data:

  • Apple — App Store distribution and the iOS platform.
  • Google — secure cloud, analytics, crash reporting, and AI processing when you use writing suggestions.

Your rights and choices

Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:

  • Ask what information we hold about you.
  • Ask us to correct or delete information (for example a problem report you sent).
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, where the law allows.
  • Withdraw consent where we rely on consent (we will explain when that applies).
  • Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, email support@masterfabric.com. We will respond within a reasonable time as required by law.

You can also stop using the app, remove AI Keyboard from Settings → General → Keyboard, or delete the app from your iPhone at any time.

If you are in the United States, we do not “sell” or “share” personal information for cross-context behavioral advertising as those terms are often defined under state privacy laws.

How long we keep information

We keep information only as long as needed to run the app, comply with law, resolve disputes, and keep the service secure. When we no longer need it, we delete or anonymize it where possible.

Where your information is processed

We and our providers may process information in countries other than yours, including the United States and countries where Google operates. We take steps designed to protect your information when it is transferred.

Children

AI Keyboard is not directed at children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has given us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Subscriptions and payments

The app does not currently offer in-app subscriptions or paid features from Masterfabric. If we add them later, we will update this policy and follow Apple’s App Store rules for payments.

Changes to this policy

We may update this page from time to time. We will change the “Last updated” date at the top. Important changes may also be communicated in the app or on this site where appropriate.

Contact

Questions about privacy: Support or support@masterfabric.com.
