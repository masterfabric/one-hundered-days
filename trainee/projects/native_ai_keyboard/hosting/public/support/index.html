Need help with AI Keyboard on your iPhone? Start below or email us—we usually need your iOS version and a short description of what happened.

Send feedback from the app

  1. Open the AI Keyboard app (the app icon on your Home Screen).
  2. Tap Report a problem.
  3. Describe what went wrong in a few sentences and send.

You can send one report per day from each device. Make sure Wi‑Fi or mobile data is turned on.

Email support

Write to support@masterfabric.com for:

  • Bugs or AI features not working
  • Privacy questions or requests about your data
  • Anything you could not resolve in the app

Add the keyboard (first time)

  1. Install AI Keyboard from the App Store and open it once.
  2. On your iPhone go to Settings → General → Keyboard → Keyboards.
  3. Tap Add New Keyboard and choose AI Keyboard.
  4. Tap AI Keyboard in the list, then turn on Allow Full Access (needed for online AI).
  5. In Messages or another app, tap the globe or keyboard icon until you see AI Keyboard.

AI suggestions not appearing?

  • Check that Allow Full Access is on for AI Keyboard.
  • Open the main app once after installing.
  • Confirm you have an internet connection.
  • Make sure you selected AI Keyboard (not only Apple’s default keyboard).
